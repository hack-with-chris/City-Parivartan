How to Use This App — Step by Step

1

Open the Report Form

Click Report an Issue from the homepage to open the report modal or page.

2

Capture Clear Evidence

Take a clear photo or short video of the issue.

3

Choose Location

Use the map picker or enter the address to pinpoint location.

4

Select Category & Add Details

Pick category (Roads, Garbage, etc.) and describe the issue.

5

Submit & Get an ID

Submit the report — you’ll receive a complaint ID.

6

Engage the Community

Share the complaint link for others to like/comment.

7

Track & Verify Resolution

Use the complaint ID to check status and verify when done.
